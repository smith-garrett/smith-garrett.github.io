About me

I am a postdoc in Shravan Vasisth's lab at the University of Potsdam working on building and testing models of sentence comprehension. As a part of the Collaborative Research Center 1287, I work on integrating such models with a computational model of eye-movement control during reading. I got my Ph.D. from the Psychology department at the University of Connecticut. Before that, I completed my Master's degree in Linguistics at the Institute for German Language and Linguistics at the Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin.
